About us

UBC Envision is a student-led think-tank that provides prospective engineers with a holistic and supportive environment to take their ideas from bench to trench. UBC Envision members foster a fearless, no-holds-barred approach when coming up with innovative solutions and contribute life-enhancing ideas to the global community.

Our Projects

At UBC Envision, our projects not only put novel science and technology developments into practice, but are moonshot solutions to some of society's challenges. From building homemade beer breweries to prototyping affordable biofuel testing and production means, Envision projects provide a channel to guide our ideas towards reality, and feed our drive to push ourselves, and the science we love, towards a greater vision.
