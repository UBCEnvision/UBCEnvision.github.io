Incident Reporting

Fill out the Google form below to report a loss incident. The main goal behind near loss reporting is to document all those incidents in a systematic manner, make sure that the team learns from their mistakes and minimize the risk of similar incidents happening in the future. A loss incident can be anything from a safety incident or near miss to an incident that resulted in lost time or finances. This could include the finance team not submitting a report on time and missed the PAF application deadline; resulting in the team losing funding for that year, or the mech team ordering the wrong equipment or supplies; resulting in delays in project completion and wasting resources.
